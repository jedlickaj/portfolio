Into the Realm of Galaxy Jellyfish

Also known as
Aurelia Aurita Galaxias

The even more extraordinary Galaxy Jellyfish species was just recently discovered. They have been observed floating around the universe outside the Milky Way. The jellyfish seem to be a peaceful species, feeding on asteroids and small planets. They are nomadic, traveling in packs to wherever the universe flow takes them.
